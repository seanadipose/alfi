William martin

co-founder
William Martin is his name, photography is his game. Martin is a New York City-based artist, designer and professional photographer that focuses his work on the face of the public. Partnered with Fake Orange Collective, he has surfaced other valuable brands in the city and beyond to be recognized in the streets.Not only is he the city’s photographer, but Martin also goes by the pseudonym Machromattica, a project that produces beautiful eccentric, opera-based tracks on Soundcloud. The atmosphere that surrounds him brings him to the soundboard to create lively beats time and time again.Follow William Martin on Instagram @williamedwardmartin and on Soundcloud @Machromattica

Philip Plotnicki

copyrighter, project nanager
Born in Montreal, Canada, located everywhere, Philip Plotnicki is a jack of all trades in the content field. His growing client base allowed him to solely work on benefiting those who need a bit of color in their branding.Plotnicki has been honored to work with top online publications and startups, as well as co-produce hip-hop mixes on his Soundcloud called NO ARTIST.
